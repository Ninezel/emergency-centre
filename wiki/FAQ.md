# FAQ

## Does Emergency Centre require accounts?

No. The open-source baseline is public-first and does not require login.

## Does it include live provider data out of the box?

Partly. The built-in starter zones now include live provider routes backed by official public sources, and the demo routes are still available as a fallback. Broader operational coverage still requires more provider adapters and local verification.

## Does the built-in starter directory cover every place?

No. It is a curated bootstrap layer, not a full geocoder.

## Can I add my own coverage areas?

Yes. Manual coverage setup remains supported.

## Can I use the app without the local API?

Yes, if your briefing URLs are already public and browser-safe.

## Why keep wiki pages in the main repo too?

They act as the source of truth and make it easier to edit, review, and republish the GitHub wiki.
